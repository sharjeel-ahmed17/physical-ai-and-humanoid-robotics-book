# Week 13: Conversational Robotics

## Overview

The final week covers conversational robotics, integrating language understanding with physical robot behavior.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

- Implement natural language understanding for robots
- Connect language commands to physical actions
- Design multimodal interaction systems
- Evaluate conversational robot performance

## Content

### Vision-Language-Action Models

Modern conversational robots integrate:

- Vision systems for environmental understanding
- Language models for command interpretation
- Action planning for physical execution

### Integration Challenges

Conversational robotics requires solving:

- Grounding language in physical reality
- Handling ambiguous commands
- Maintaining context during interaction
- Ensuring safe and appropriate responses

## Course Conclusion

Congratulations on completing the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics course! You now have the foundational knowledge to develop advanced robotic systems that can function effectively in the physical world.
